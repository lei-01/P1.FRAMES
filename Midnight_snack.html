Mozzarella and Mojos

Ingredients: Mozzarella cheese, Potatoes, All-purpose flour, Cornstarch, Bread Crumbs, Eggs, Salt, Pepper, Water, and Oil

Steps:

  1. Cut the mozzarella cheese into even stick-sized pieces and wash, peel, and slice the potatoes into thick rounds.
  2. Boil the potato slices in lightly salted water until slightly tender, then drain and allow them to cool completely.
  3. Prepare three bowls by placing flour in the first bowl, beaten eggs in the second bowl, and bread crumbs mixed with salt and pepper in the third bowl.
  4. Coat each mozzarella stick in flour, dip it into the beaten eggs, and roll it in the bread crumbs, then repeat the egg and bread crumb coating once more for a thicker crust.
  5. Place the coated mozzarella sticks in the freezer for at least 30 minutes to prevent the cheese from melting too quickly during frying.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine the remaining flour, cornstarch, salt, and pepper, then add enough water to form a smooth batter for the mojos.
  7. Dip each cooled potato slice into the batter until fully coated.
  8. Heat cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  9. Fry the mozzarella sticks until golden brown, then remove and drain on paper towels.
  10. Fry the battered potato slices until crispy and golden brown, then remove and drain excess oil.
  11. Serve both mozzarella sticks and mojos hot with your preferred dipping sauces.
