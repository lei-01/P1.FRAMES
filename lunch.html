Pork Adobo

Ingredients: Prok Belly, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Peppercorn, Dried bay leaves, Vinegar, Salt.

Steps:

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes.
  3. Pour the remaining marinated including the gralic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Put the vinegar inside and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste.
