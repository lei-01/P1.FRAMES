Dessert

Mais Con Yelo

Ingredients: Sweet corn kernels, Shaved or crushed ice, Evaporated milk, Condensed milk, and Sugar

Steps:

  1. Prepare the sweet corn by draining the syrup if canned or boiling fresh corn until tender, then add a small amount of sugar while still warm if desired.
  2. Crush or shave the ice using a blender or ice crusher until fine.
  3. Place the crushed ice into a tall glass or serving bowl.
  4. Add the prepared sweet corn on top of the ice.
  5. Pour evaporated milk evenly over the ice and corn mixture.
  6. Drizzle condensed milk according to your preferred sweetness.
  7. Serve immediately and mix lightly before eating to enjoy a refreshing Mais Con Yelo.
